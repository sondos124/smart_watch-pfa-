About VitalTrack

VitalTrack is an innovative application designed to provide families with peace of mind by allowing them to monitor the health and well-being of their elderly loved ones. Our application leverages advanced technology, including pulse sensors, GPS tracking, and temperature monitoring, integrated into a wearable device known as the VitalTrack bracelet.

Our Mission

At VitalTrack, our mission is to empower families to stay connected and informed about the health status of their elderly family members. We understand the challenges and concerns associated with caring for aging loved ones, and we are committed to providing a solution that enhances the quality of life for both seniors and their families.

Key Features

  • Pulse Sensor Monitoring: VitalTrack continuously monitors the pulse rate of the wearer, providing real-time insights into their cardiovascular health.
  • GPS Tracking: With GPS technology, VitalTrack enables families to track the location of their loved ones, ensuring their safety and providing peace of mind.
  • Temperature Sensing: The temperature sensor in the VitalTrack bracelet helps detect changes in body temperature, alerting caregivers to potential health issues such as fever or hypothermia.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Our intuitive interface makes it easy for users to access and interpret vital health data, ensuring a seamless monitoring experience.

How It Works

The VitalTrack bracelet is worn by the elderly person, and the data collected by the sensors is transmitted to the VitalTrack application on the caregiver's smartphone or computer. Caregivers can view the data in real-time, receive alerts for any abnormalities, and access historical health records for trend analysis.
